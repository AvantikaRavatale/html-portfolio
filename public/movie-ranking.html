The Best Movies According to Avantika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

KGF-Chapter 1

Impressed with its gritty portrayal of ambition and power. The film's grand scale and intricate plotline,
centered on Rocky's rise in the gold mining underworld, were particularly compelling.

The Notebook

This movie is a beautifully crafted romance that spans decades. The passionate love story between
Noah and Allie, marked by their unwavering commitment despite numerous obstacles, resonates deeply
with audiences and exemplifies enduring love.

Me Before You

"Me Before You" was such a tearjerker! I loved Lou and Will's story - it was so romantic and heartbreaking at the same time.
